Re-Brew-Able. Package Design.
X
Marketing campaign for "environmentally sustainable beer". Concept and package design are in response to the wasteful practices of drinking in a college environment. The brand name plays off the word renewable; the contents in the bottles can be combined to grow a plant for food. Bottles are filled with seeds, soil, and water. Each flavor has its own unique label, copy, and promotional poster, geared towards the young consumer (21-25 year olds). All materials are recycled. This design was selected for the Penny Stamps School of Art and Design Juried Undergraduate Exhibition and received Honorable Mention. 2012.
